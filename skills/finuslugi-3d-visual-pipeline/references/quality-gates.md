# Quality Gates

## Weighted scorecard

- communication meaning: 25;
- mandatory subject features: 20;
- style compliance: 20;
- composition: 15;
- anatomy or construction: 10;
- technical quality: 10.

## Decisions

- `90-100`: accept;
- `80-89`: accept after a minor correction;
- `65-79`: mandatory revision;
- below `65`: reject the candidate.

## Critical defects

A candidate fails regardless of total score when it contains:

- wrong main object;
- lost or inverted communication meaning;
- failed identity lock;
- impossible anatomy or construction;
- severely damaged face or hands when relevant;
- wrong or generated Finuslugi logo;
- unreadable mandatory text;
- violation of a primary style invariant;
- unintended crop of the main object;
- missing user-facing image delivery.

## Gate groups

### Meaning

The metaphor, action, and product advantage read without explanation.

### Geometry

The form is physically plausible, recognizable, and consistent with the assigned subject or geometry reference.

### Composition

The hierarchy is clear, scale is correct, negative space is preserved, and adaptive cropping remains possible.

### Style

Materials, color distribution, light, background, and detail match the selected versioned style pack.

### Brand

The logo is an approved asset, the correct variant is used for the background, and brand red is `#FF0508` where specified.

### Technical

No watermarks, accidental pseudo-text, broken edges, severe artifacts, unintended transparency, or inadequate resolution.
